Tank Transfers

How do I use this page?
  • To transfer between tanks, click the tank to transfer from, and then the tank to transfer to.
  • Click 'External' and then click a tank to fill that tank.
  • Click a tank and then click 'External' to empty that tank.
  • Use your mousewheel to zoom in and out, click and drag the floor to move around.
